Some 9-patch guidance

In order for the dark arts of 9-patch to work please follow these:

  1. Draw 4 black colored patches and group them
  2. Group your content (might be best to include an actual slice or a mask inside it to make sure its size is as you want it)
  3. Group the 2 groups and name that group after your slice, ending with a ".9"
